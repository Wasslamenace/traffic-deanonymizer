# Traffic De-Anonymizer

**Turn Anonymous Website Visitors Into CRM Leads Automatically**

Done-for-you Zapier/Make.com workflows that identify companies visiting your site, enrich with data, and push to your CRM.

---

## The Problem

**90% of your website visitors are anonymous numbers.**

You're spending money on ads, SEO, content... but most visitors leave without a trace. You have no idea which companies are interested, what they looked at, or when to follow up.

---

## The Solution

**Hybrid Service → SaaS Approach**

### Phase 1: Done-For-You Service (Launch: 10 mars)
- Custom Zapier/Make.com workflow setup
- Company identification (IP → Company lookup)
- Data enrichment (Clearbit, Hunter.io)
- CRM integration (HubSpot, Pipedrive, Google Sheets)
- Slack alerts for hot leads
- **Price**: $200-500 one-time setup

### Phase 2: SaaS Product (Launch: 16 avril IF validated)
- Self-serve signup
- Real-time dashboard
- Automated enrichment
- Multi-CRM sync
- **Price**: $49-99/month recurring

## How It Works

```
Visitor → Identify (IP → Company) → Enrich (Clearbit) → Score → Push to CRM → Slack Alert
```

**Tech Stack**:
- Zapier/Make.com (automation)
- Clearbit Reveal (IP → Company, $0.01/lookup)
- Hunter.io (email enrichment, $49/mo)
- HubSpot/Pipedrive/Sheets (CRM)
- Slack (alerts)
