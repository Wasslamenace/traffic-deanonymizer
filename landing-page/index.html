FAQ

How does visitor identification work?

We use IP-to-company databases (Clearbit, Hunter.io) to match visitor IPs to registered businesses. ~10% of website traffic is identifiable (B2B companies, not individuals).

What CRMs do you support?

HubSpot, Pipedrive, Salesforce, Zoho, Attio, Airtable, Google Sheets, or any tool with Zapier/webhook support.

Do I need to pay for enrichment APIs?

Yes. Clearbit (~$50/mo), Hunter.io (~$49/mo). You'll also need Zapier ($20-50/mo depending on volume). We help you set this up.

How long does setup take?

Basic: 2-4 hours. Pro: 4-6 hours. Enterprise: Custom timeline. We deliver in 1-3 business days after you provide access.

What if I don't have a CRM?

No problem. We'll set up a Google Sheets dashboard (free) as your CRM. You can upgrade to HubSpot/Pipedrive later.

Can you add lead scoring?

Yes (Pro/Enterprise packages). We score based on company size, pages visited, traffic source, and custom rules you define.
