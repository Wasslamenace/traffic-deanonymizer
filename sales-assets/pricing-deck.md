## 📊 The Problem

**90% of your website visitors are anonymous numbers.**

You're spending money on ads, SEO, content... but most visitors leave without a trace.

**Pain points**:

- Can't follow up (no contact info)

**Result**: Wasted traffic, lost revenue.

## ✅ The Solution

**Done-For-You Automation**:
- Identify companies visiting your site (IP → Company lookup)
- Enrich with data (size, industry, tech stack)
- Score leads (hot/warm/cold based on behavior)
- Push to your CRM automatically
- Alert sales team (Slack, email, SMS)

**No code. No maintenance. Just leads.**
